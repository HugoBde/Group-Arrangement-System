Allocator

Your questions answered

If you've got any questions about Allocator Inc, read on below for answers to some of the most common questions. If you can't find an answer, you can contact your tutor regarding it. Additionally, some of the FAQ's can be found on the home page

Can I change my group after being allocated?

No. In most cases, this isn't possible, but, in certain circumstances your teacher might allow you to. Contact, your teacher regarding this.

Can I change my preferences?

Yes, you are able to choose and update your preferences. Go to Dashboard > Preferences, choose your preference and submit. Notify your teacher before choosing a new preference.

How does the group allocation work?

We use a grouping algorithm based on interests created by your teacher you can select from, your background, expertise. All of these are taken into account and places you into groups with other students.

Can others see my preferences?

Other students can not view your preferences but it can be viewed by your teacher. This is to ensure confidentiality and make the grouping process unbiased and fair.

How long does it take to be placed in a group?

You're placed in your group as soon as your teacher has decided it is time to be placed in a group.

What happens after I'm allocated to a team?

Your teacher will provide further instructions as soon as you're allocated. However, it is advised to get in touch with your team and create/organise a communication channel.
